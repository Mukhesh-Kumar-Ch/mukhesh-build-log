### Notes

#### Frequency Analysis

* Counts occurrences of each word in a document.
* Frequently occurring words often indicate the main topic.
* Simple but cannot distinguish common words from important words.

#### TF-IDF (Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency)

* Identifies words important to a specific document.
* High TF-IDF = frequent in one document, rare across others.
* Common words like "AI" or "Microsoft" usually get low scores.

#### Bag-of-Words (BoW)

* Represents text as word-frequency vectors.
* Ignores grammar and word order.
* Used in spam detection, sentiment analysis, and text classification.

#### TextRank

* Graph-based algorithm inspired by PageRank.
* Ranks sentences based on similarity to other sentences.
* Used for keyword extraction and extractive text summarization.

### Takeaways

* Frequency Analysis → Finds common topics.
* TF-IDF → Finds unique and important keywords.
* Bag-of-Words → Converts text into ML-friendly numerical features.
* TextRank → Automatically generates summaries from important sentences.
